Case Study

Problem

People hear “rates up 0.50%” but can’t translate that into monthly budget impact, total interest, or the risk of future resets (especially for ARMs).

Solution

  • Baseline vs scenario calculator across loan types
  • Stress testing: fixed steps or ARM behavior
  • ARM model: fixed period, index + margin, periodic + lifetime caps
  • Time-varying index: Treasury history or manual schedules
  • Exportable artifacts: share link, CSV, PDF report

Implementation Highlights

  • Amortization engine with principal/interest split and payoff timing
  • Stress simulation that re-amortizes at each reset
  • Robust data fetch strategy with best-effort proxy fallback
  • Professional UX: multi-page layout, responsive navigation, consistent design system
