APR

APR is the yearly interest rate. We convert it to a monthly rate for calculations.

PITI

For mortgages, many budgets use PITI: Principal + Interest + Taxes + Insurance (+ HOA).

Total interest

Total interest can be huge over long terms. Small APR changes can add up.

Extra payments

Extra payments reduce principal faster and cut interest costs.
