About

RateSense helps you understand how interest-rate changes affect real costs: monthly payments, total interest, payoff time, and (on Stress Test) how payments could change if rates move over time.

What RateSense does

Calculator

Enter your balance, rate, and loan length. RateSense shows the baseline result and what changes under a selected rate-change scenario.

Open Calculator

Compare

Compare two loan options side-by-side (payment, total interest, payoff time, and for mortgages, total monthly housing cost when you include taxes/insurance/fees).

Open Compare

Stress Test

Simulate rising-rate paths and see the highest monthly payment risk over time.

Open Stress Test

How to use it

Calculator

  1. Select a loan type.
  2. Enter your loan balance and annual interest rate.
  3. For amortized loans, enter the loan length in years (and optional extra monthly payment).
  4. Select a rate-change scenario (example: +0.50%).
  5. Click Calculate.

Mortgage housing cost

If you add property taxes, homeowner’s insurance, and monthly fees, RateSense shows your total monthly housing cost:

total housing cost = payment + (annual taxes ÷ 12) + (annual insurance ÷ 12) + monthly fees

Important notes

  • Estimates: Results are math estimates based on what you enter.
  • Real loans vary: Fees, escrow rules, and terms can change real outcomes.
  • Not financial advice: Informational use only.
