About RateSense

RateSense helps you understand what interest-rate changes mean in real dollars: monthly payments, total interest, payoff timing, and “payment shock” risk over time.

How to use it

Calculator steps

  1. Select a loan type.
  2. Enter balance, interest rate, and loan length (if applicable).
  3. Choose a rate-change scenario (example: +0.50%).
  4. Click Calculate.

Mortgage housing cost

If you add property taxes, insurance, and monthly fees, RateSense shows total monthly housing cost:

Total housing cost = monthly loan payment + (annual taxes ÷ 12) + (annual insurance ÷ 12) + monthly fees

What the results mean

Monthly payment

The scheduled principal + interest payment for the loan.

Total interest

The total interest paid over the payoff period under your inputs.

Payoff time

How many months it takes to pay off the balance.

Important notes

  • Estimates: Results are mathematical estimates based on what you enter.
  • Real loans vary: Fees, escrow rules, and terms can change real outcomes.
  • Not financial advice: This tool is educational/informational only.
